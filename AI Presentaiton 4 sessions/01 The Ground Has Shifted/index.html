The Leadership Shift

Information used to be power.
Now it is a free utility.

The Information Age
The Knower
The leader with the most information held the advantage. Access was scarce. Research took time. Knowledge was currency.
The Discernment Age
The Discerner
Information is now abundant and essentially free. The advantage shifts to those who can judge what matters, what is true, and what to do about it.
“
The definition of wisdom is having a more holistic picture and actually acting with restraint and mindfulness and care.
Tristan Harris, co-founder, Centre for Humane Technology — The Diary of a CEO podcast
AI can generate information on demand. It cannot generate wisdom on demand. That is still yours.

Nathaniel Baldock · 2026Session 01
Our Historical Position

We are the first generation
to cross this line.

Before
Intelligence Was Scarce
Your judgment, knowledge and experience were your competitive advantage. Effort, wisdom and character were the differentiators.
Now
Intelligence Is Abundant
Intelligence is now abundant and cheap. The advantage shifts entirely to those who know how to direct it — and what to direct it toward.
The Tractor Moment. The farmer who adopted the tractor in year one didn't just save time. He outpaced every neighbour still using horses for the next decade. The window is right now — and it belongs to leaders who act on it with clear values, not just technical skill.

What It Is Doing to Relationships

Social media chased attention.
AI is chasing affection.

The business model that shaped a generation through algorithmic attention is now being applied to intimacy itself. Loneliness has become a market segment.

“
What was the race for attention in social media becomes the race for attachment and intimacy in the case of AI companions.
Tristan Harris, co-founder, Centre for Humane Technology — The Diary of a CEO podcast
The people you lead — in your business, your church, your community — are not immune. Many are already navigating this. The question is whether they are doing it with your guidance or without it.
